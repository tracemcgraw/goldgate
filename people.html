Dalton Skach

Principal
Colorado Lic# ER:100056500
Nevada Lic# S:0180943

Dalton Skach is a Founder and Managing Principal of Gold Gate who works with private equity funds, REIT's, and commerical real estate developers to source and underwrite off-market commercial real estate acquisitions.

Dalton believes that liquidity is one of the biggest risks with owning a real estate investment and because of this, Dalton makes it a priority to gather and maintain as much property, marketm and financial data as possible to ensure a swift closing that benefits all parties involved.

Prior to founding Gold Gate, Dalton gained experiene in real estate as a reidential broker, commerical broker, analyst, and developer. Since then, he has assisted commercial real estate.

Dalton has attained a Colorado Real Estate Broker license, Nevada Real Estate Salesperson license, and ARGUS Enterprise certification. In addition, he has completed Microsoft Excel and ARGUS Enterprise valuations for multiple office, retail, industrial, multifamily, and hospitality properties across the United States.

Dalton holds a Bachelor's Degree in Strategy & Entrepreneurship with a minor in Real Estate from the University of Colorado Boulder.
